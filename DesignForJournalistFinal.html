WHO

New Story, a non-profit working to end global homelessness, has partnered with Icon, a homebuilding robotics company, to create the world’s first 3D printed community.

LEARN MORE

WHAT

24 hours to build. $6,000 for one home. Three times stronger than concrete. Zero waste.The first version of the printer, Vulcan 1, was unveiled at South by Southwest in Austin, TX, in March 2018. In 2019, a modified and improved version, Vulcan 2, will print the world’s first 3D printed community.

LEARN MORE

WHY

The challenge the charity faces is massive. Over a billion people are in need of homes, and the number grows year by year. To find a solution, the young company decided they have to do more than simply continue building. They have to build better, faster, and more affordably than ever before.
